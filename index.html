About Us

MyIntellX Plt, is a Kuala Lumpur-based pioneer in artificial intelligence and cloud integration, offering advanced solutions such as end-to-end AI platform deployment, data science analytics, software engineering with agile methodologies, cybersecurity, and IT training. Driven by over 25 years of global expertise of founders and successful collaborations with leading enterprises, MyIntellX delivers impactful innovations—including Malaysia’s first offline language model, cutting-edge AI on chip solutions, and multi-industry data platforms—that have generated multimillion-dollar savings and empowered organizations across manufacturing, aviation, telecom, finance, and beyond to realize scalable growth, operational excellence, and future-ready capabilities.
